ADR-008: Continuous Evidence

Version: 1.0
Status: Accepted
Author: Marvin G. Dewitt

Context

Audit preparation often requires manually collecting evidence from SharePoint, exports, screenshots, tickets, logs, reports, and spreadsheets.

This creates high effort and increases the chance of stale or missing evidence.

Decision

USOP will treat evidence as a continuously maintained operational object.

Evidence will be created, linked, refreshed, and evaluated through normal security workflows.

Consequences

Evidence is mapped to controls, frameworks, tasks, journal entries, risks, POA&Ms, policies, and procedures.

Audit readiness becomes a byproduct of daily security operations.

Product Principle

Evidence should be generated continuously, not collected periodically.
