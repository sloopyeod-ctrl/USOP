ADR-006: Human-Centered Security Operations

Version: 1.0
Status: Accepted
Author: Marvin G. Dewitt

Context

Security Engineers think in terms of work, investigations, reviews, approvals, and decisions.

Traditional tools often organize around modules, tables, or compliance frameworks instead of operational workflows.

Decision

USOP will be designed around human workflows.

Mission Control will present what needs attention, why it matters, and what should happen next.

Consequences

The user experience prioritizes tasks, reviews, evidence health, relationship context, and operational clarity.

Dashboards should support decisions, not create noise.

Product Principle

USOP should answer: “What should I work on next?”
