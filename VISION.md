# USOP Vision

## Operational Intelligence for Modern Security Teams

USOP exists to reduce operational friction for Security Engineers.

Security teams should not spend their time chasing spreadsheets, searching SharePoint, exporting CSVs, reconciling accounts, or rebuilding audit evidence from scratch.

They should spend their time improving security.

---

## North Star

Operational Clarity Through Continuous Intelligence

---

## What USOP Does

USOP connects authoritative enterprise systems, normalizes operational data, understands relationships, identifies meaningful changes, generates workflows, preserves knowledge, creates evidence, and presents clarity through Mission Control.

---

## What USOP Is Not

USOP is not a SIEM.

USOP is not a GRC tool.

USOP is not a CMDB.

USOP is not an IAM platform.

USOP is the operational intelligence layer above those systems.

---

## Guiding Beliefs

Security work should flow.

Automation should propose; engineers should approve.

Relationships matter more than isolated records.

Evidence should be generated continuously.

Compliance should be the result of strong security operations.

Knowledge should be preserved before it becomes tribal knowledge.

---

## Product Promise

Know what changed.

Know why it matters.

Know what to do next.
